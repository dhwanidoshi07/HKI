About Us

H. K. International is one of the leading manufacturers, exporters, suppliers, and stockists for all types of Fasteners and Gaskets and hardware items in all grades of material. We are established to design and manufacture stud bolts, nuts, foundation bolts, U-Clamps, collar bolts, carriage bolts, scaffolding clamps & accessories, and all types of special customized fasteners & gaskets like spiral wound, ASB./ Non Asb. ring type, etc., for industrial, refineries, and petrochemical plants and onshore-offshore installations.
Quality Assurance
With 22 years of rich Technical knowledge (Since 1988), we have been manufacturing these products with carefully selected material of the finest metallurgical quality. The control of the production and materials by stringent applications of Quality Assurance is strictly adhered to.
